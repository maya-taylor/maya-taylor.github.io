Arduino Shield for UBC Rocket Payload

Project Description:

  • The Arduino Shield manages voltage readings, temperature sensors, and data logging within the Microbial Fuel Cell (MFC) payload of the UBC Rocket 2024 project.
  • Designed in AutoDesk Fusion, it features LED indicators for data writing status and a safety switch for SD card power control.
  • Temperature sensors and fuel cells are connected to the board via screw terminals.
  • Regular updates and revisions optimize performance and ensure fit within the rocket's payload bay.
  • Documented iterations of PCB drafts and Bill of Materials (BOM) updates track the project's progress.
  • The project demonstrates practical engineering application in aerospace contexts, emphasizing problem-solving and collaboration.
